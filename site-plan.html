Content

Home page

The purpose of this website will be for Windfall Property Investment employees to have at their fingertips information about their property, loan, and purchase information. The Home page will be the employee profiles and responsibilities. In other words, it will be the "About Us" page for now. It is the homepage because the uses of this website will only be internal with a log-in required.

Images for the Home page

Properties

Page 2 will consist of property information. It will contain the image of the property and then beneath the image will be text about the property information & the Loan and Puchase Infromation. Under Property Information will contain Bedrooms, Bathrooms, Square feet, Yr. Built. The Loan & Purchase Information will contain Purchase Date, Purchase Price, Loan Amount, Loan Term, Loan Interest Rate, Lender, and Monthly Payment.
